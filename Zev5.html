Features & Traits

Race: Simic Hybrid

Animal Enhancement (Level 1): Grappling Appendages

You have two powerful, semi-prehensile tendrils. You can use a bonus action to try to grapple a creature within 10 feet. You have advantage on all Strength (Athletics) checks made to grapple.

Animal Enhancement (Level 5): Carapace

Your AC becomes 13 + your Dexterity modifier when you aren't wearing armor. You can use a shield and still gain this benefit.

Bard Features

Bardic Inspiration (d8)

You have 4 inspiration dice per short or long rest. As a bonus action, you can inspire a creature within 60 feet, giving them a d8 they can add to one ability check, attack roll, or saving throw within the next 10 minutes.

Jack of All Trades

Add half your proficiency bonus (+1) to any ability check you make that doesn't already include your proficiency bonus.

Song of Rest (d6)

Creatures who spend Hit Dice to heal during a short rest with you regain an extra 1d6 HP.

Expertise

Your proficiency bonus is doubled for Persuasion and Athletics.

Font of Inspiration

You regain all expended uses of Bardic Inspiration when you finish a short or long rest.

College of Eloquence

Silver Tongue

When you make a Charisma (Persuasion) or Charisma (Deception) check, you can treat any roll of 9 or lower on the d20 as a 10. Your minimum Persuasion check is 20 (10 + 10 mod) and your minimum Deception check is 17 (10 + 7 mod).

Unsettling Words

As a bonus action, you can expend one use of your Bardic Inspiration. Choose one creature you can see within 60 feet. Roll the Bardic Inspiration die (d8) and subtract the number rolled from the target's next saving throw before your next turn.

Cantrips (3 Known)

  • Vicious Mockery: Target makes Wisdom save or takes 2d4 psychic damage and has disadvantage on its next attack roll. Her primary offensive cantrip—cutting remarks that wound and weaken. Perfect to use while grappling someone.
  • Mage Hand: Create a spectral hand that can manipulate objects, open unlocked doors, or retrieve items from up to 30 feet away. Essential utility for interacting with strange technology from a safe distance.
  • Light: Touch an object to make it shed bright light in a 20-foot radius and dim light for an additional 20 feet for 1 hour. A simple but crucial utility spell for navigating dark corridors and alien landscapes.

Spells Known (8 Total)

1st Level Spells:

  • Charm Person: Make a humanoid view you as a friendly acquaintance for 1 hour. The quintessential spell for infiltration and information gathering.
  • Dissonant Whispers: Whisper a terrible secret that deals 3d6 psychic damage and forces the target to use its reaction to move away from you. Perfect for battlefield control.
  • Healing Word: As a bonus action, restore 1d4+4 hit points to a creature within 60 feet. Bring downed allies back into the fight while keeping your action free.
  • Heroism: Touch a creature to grant immunity to fear and 4 temporary hit points each round for 1 minute (concentration). Great support spell to bolster allies.

2nd Level Spells:

  • Calm Emotions: Suppress strong emotions in a 20-foot-radius sphere, ending the charmed or frightened conditions and suppressing hostile emotions. Perfect for de-escalating tense situations.
  • Suggestion: Suggest a course of activity (limited to a sentence or two) and magically influence a creature to follow that course for up to 8 hours. Incredibly powerful for manipulation and social encounters.

3rd Level Spells:

  • Hypnotic Pattern: Create a mesmerizing pattern in a 30-foot cube. Creatures that fail a Wisdom save are charmed and incapacitated. Top-tier battlefield control—incapacitate entire groups of hostiles.
  • Fear: Project phantasmal images of creatures' worst fears in a 30-foot cone. Frightened creatures must flee. An excellent \"get off me\" spell to disrupt enemy formations.
