FEATURES & TRAITS

Racial Traits (Human - Variant)

  • +1 Charisma (already applied to ability score)

Class Features (Rogue - Level 5)

Expertise: Your proficiency bonus is doubled for Deception and Arcana checks.

Sneak Attack (3d6): Once per turn, you can deal an extra 3d6 damage to one creature you hit with an attack if you have advantage on the attack roll. The attack must use a Finesse or a ranged weapon. You don't need advantage on the attack roll if another enemy of the target is within 5 feet of it, that enemy isn't incapacitated, and you don't have disadvantage on the attack roll.

Thieves' Cant: You know the secret slang of disgruntled functionaries, allowing you to pass messages in seemingly mundane bureaucratic complaints. During your rogue training you learned thieves' cant, a secret mix of dialect, jargon, and code that allows you to hide messages in seemingly normal conversation.

Cunning Action: Your quick thinking allows you to take a bonus action on each of your turns in combat. This action can be used only to take the Dash, Disengage, or Hide action. Perfect for getting out of the way.

✨ NEW - Uncanny Dodge: Starting at 5th level, when an attacker that you can see hits you with an attack, you can use your reaction to halve the attack's damage against you. This is the core of Stanley's impossible luck, allowing him to survive blows that should have disintegrated him.

Roguish Archetype: Mastermind

Master of Intrigue: You gain proficiency with the disguise kit, the forgery kit, and one gaming set of your choice. You also learn two languages of your choice (Trade Cant, Insectoid).

Master of Tactics: You can use the Help action as a bonus action. When you use the Help action to aid an ally in attacking a creature, the target of that attack can be within 30 feet of you, rather than 5 feet of you, if the target can see or hear you. This lets you coordinate your allies from a safe distance—exactly where Stanley prefers to be.

Background: Functionary (Custom)

Feature: Bureaucratic Navigation

You have an instinctive understanding of bureaucratic systems and hierarchies. You can usually predict how officials will react to requests, identify which forms or procedures are needed, and recognize opportunities to exploit red tape or delay enforcement. When dealing with governmental or organizational systems, you have advantage on checks to navigate regulations, forge documents, or talk your way past minor officials.

Skill Proficiencies: Investigation, Sleight of Hand

Tool Proficiencies: Forgery Kit, one Gaming Set

Languages: Two of your choice (Trade Cant, Insectoid)

✨ Level 4 Feat: Observant

Your paranoid hypervigilance gives you an edge. You gain the following benefits:

  • Increase your Wisdom score by 1 (from 13 to 14).
  • You can read lips of any creature you can see that speaks a language you know.
  • You gain a +5 bonus to your passive Wisdom (Perception) and passive Intelligence (Investigation) scores.

Rogue Feature: Expertise

Your proficiency bonus is doubled for the following skills:

  • Deception
  • Arcana

Rogue Feature: Sneak Attack (3d6)

Once per turn, you can deal an extra 3d6 damage to one creature you hit with an attack if you have advantage on the attack roll. The attack must use a finesse or a ranged weapon.

You don't need advantage on the attack roll if another enemy of the target is within 5 feet of it, that enemy isn't incapacitated, and you don't have disadvantage on the attack roll.

Rogue Feature: Thieves' Cant

You know thieves' cant, a secret mix of dialect, jargon, and code that allows you to hide messages in seemingly normal conversation.

Rogue Feature: Cunning Action

You can take a bonus action on each of your turns in combat to take the Dash, Disengage, or Hide action.

Mastermind Feature: Master of Intrigue

You gain proficiency with the disguise kit, the forgery kit, one gaming set of your choice, and two languages of your choice.

Mastermind Feature: Master of Tactics

You can use the Help action as a bonus action. Additionally, when you use the Help action to aid an ally in attacking a creature, the target of that attack can be within 30 feet of you, rather than within 5 feet of you, if the target can see or hear you.

This is Stanley's core ability: cowering behind cover while pointing out a weakness, giving an ally advantage on their next attack.

Rogue Feature: Uncanny Dodge

When an attacker that you can see hits you with an attack, you can use your reaction to halve the attack's damage against you.

This represents Stanley's impossible luck, allowing him to survive blows that should have disintegrated him.

PERSONALITY

Personality Traits: Cowardly, self-preserving, surprisingly resourceful when cornered. Constantly complaining but somehow keeps surviving.

Ideals: Survival above all else. The universe is cruel and unfair, so the only thing that matters is staying alive.

Bonds: Despite his cowardice, Stanley forms unexpected attachments to the few people who don't immediately try to kill or exploit him.

Flaws: Extreme cowardice. Will lie, cheat, or hide to avoid danger. Terrible at making decisions under pressure.
