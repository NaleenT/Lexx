FEATURES & TRAITS

Racial Traits (Human - Variant)

Skills: Perception (proficiency)

Feat: Skill Expert

  • +1 Charisma (already applied to ability score)
  • Gain proficiency in Stealth
  • Gain Expertise in Insight (represents your desperate, paranoid need to read every guard's mood and every superior's intention)

Class Features (Rogue - Level 3)

Expertise: Your proficiency bonus is doubled for Deception and Arcana checks.

Sneak Attack (2d6): Once per turn, you can deal an extra 2d6 damage to one creature you hit with an attack if you have advantage on the attack roll. The attack must use a Finesse or a ranged weapon. You don't need advantage on the attack roll if another enemy of the target is within 5 feet of it, that enemy isn't incapacitated, and you don't have disadvantage on the attack roll.

Thieves' Cant: You know the secret slang of disgruntled functionaries, allowing you to pass messages in seemingly mundane bureaucratic complaints. During your rogue training you learned thieves' cant, a secret mix of dialect, jargon, and code that allows you to hide messages in seemingly normal conversation.

Cunning Action: Your quick thinking allows you to take a bonus action on each of your turns in combat. This action can be used only to take the Dash, Disengage, or Hide action. Perfect for getting out of the way.

Roguish Archetype: Mastermind

Master of Intrigue: You gain proficiency with the disguise kit, the forgery kit, and one gaming set of your choice. You also learn two languages of your choice (Trade Cant, Insectoid).

Master of Tactics: You can use the Help action as a bonus action. When you use the Help action to aid an ally in attacking a creature, the target of that attack can be within 30 feet of you, rather than 5 feet of you, if the target can see or hear you. This lets you coordinate your allies from a safe distance—exactly where Stanley prefers to be.

Background: Functionary (Custom)

Feature: Bureaucratic Navigation

You have an instinctive understanding of bureaucratic systems and hierarchies. You can usually predict how officials will react to requests, identify which forms or procedures are needed, and recognize opportunities to exploit red tape or delay enforcement. When dealing with governmental or organizational systems, you have advantage on checks to navigate regulations, forge documents, or talk your way past minor officials.

Skill Proficiencies: Investigation, Sleight of Hand

Tool Proficiencies: Forgery Kit, one Gaming Set

Languages: Two of your choice (Trade Cant, Insectoid)

PERSONALITY

Personality Traits: Cowardly, self-preserving, surprisingly resourceful when cornered. Constantly complaining but somehow keeps surviving.

Ideals: Survival above all else. The universe is cruel and unfair, so the only thing that matters is staying alive.

Bonds: Despite his cowardice, Stanley forms unexpected attachments to the few people who don't immediately try to kill or exploit him.

Flaws: Extreme cowardice. Will lie, cheat, or hide to avoid danger. Terrible at making decisions under pressure.
