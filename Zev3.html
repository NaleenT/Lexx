Features & Traits

Race: Simic Hybrid

Animal Enhancement (Level 1): Grappling Appendages

You have two powerful, semi-prehensile tendrils. You can use a bonus action to try to grapple a creature within 10 feet. You have advantage on all Strength (Athletics) checks made to grapple.

Bard Features

Bardic Inspiration (d6)

You have 3 inspiration dice per long rest. As a bonus action, you can inspire a creature, giving them a d6 they can add to one ability check, attack roll, or saving throw.

Jack of All Trades

You can add half your proficiency bonus (+1) to any ability check you make that doesn't already include your proficiency bonus.

Song of Rest (d6)

Creatures who spend Hit Dice to heal during a short rest with you regain an extra 1d6 HP.

Expertise

Your proficiency bonus is doubled for Persuasion and Athletics.

Bard Archetype: College of Eloquence

Silver Tongue

When you make a Charisma (Persuasion) or Charisma (Deception) check, you can treat any roll of 9 or lower on the d20 as a 10.

Unsettling Words

As a bonus action, you can expend one use of your Bardic Inspiration. Choose one creature you can see within 60 feet. Roll the Bardic Inspiration die and subtract the number rolled from the target's next saving throw before your next turn.

1st Level Spells

  • Charm Person: Make a humanoid view you as a friendly acquaintance for 1 hour. The quintessential spell for infiltration and information gathering.
  • Dissonant Whispers: Whisper a terrible secret that deals 3d6 psychic damage and forces the target to use its reaction to move away from you. Perfect for battlefield control and synergizes with Unsettling Words.
  • Healing Word: As a bonus action, restore 1d4+3 hit points to a creature within 60 feet. Bring downed allies back into the fight while keeping your action free.
  • Heroism: Touch a creature to grant immunity to fear and temporary hit points equal to your Charisma modifier each round for 1 minute. Great support spell to bolster allies.

2nd Level Spells

  • Calm Emotions: Suppress strong emotions in a 20-foot radius, potentially ending a fight before it starts or negating charm/fear effects on allies. A powerful tool for de-escalation.
  • Suggestion: Suggest a course of action to a creature, which must obey if it fails a Wisdom save (provided the suggestion sounds reasonable). Lasts up to 8 hours. Pairs incredibly well with Silver Tongue.
