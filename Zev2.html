Race: Simic Hybrid

Animal Enhancement (Level 1): Grappling Appendages

You have two powerful, semi-prehensile tendrils. You can use a bonus action to try to grapple a creature within 10 feet. You have advantage on all Strength (Athletics) checks made to grapple.

Bard Features

Bardic Inspiration (d6)

You have 3 inspiration dice per long rest. As a bonus action, you can inspire a creature within 60 feet, giving them a d6 they can add to one ability check, attack roll, or saving throw within the next 10 minutes.
