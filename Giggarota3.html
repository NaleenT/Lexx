FEATURES & TRAITS

Racial Traits (Orc)

Darkvision (60 ft.): See in dim light as if bright, darkness as if dim light.

Aggressive: Bonus action to move up to your speed toward a hostile creature you can see.

Powerful Build: Count as one size larger for carrying capacity and weight you can push, drag, or lift.

Class Features (Barbarian Level 3)

Rage - "Feeding Frenzy" (3/Long Rest): Bonus action to rage for 1 minute. Gain advantage on Strength checks and saves, +2 melee damage with Strength weapons, resistance to bludgeoning/piercing/slashing damage.

Rage Tracker:

Unarmored Defense: AC = 10 + DEX modifier + CON modifier (when not wearing armor).

Reckless Attack: You can give yourself advantage on melee weapon attack rolls using Strength during your turn, but attack rolls against you have advantage until your next turn.

Danger Sense: You have advantage on Dexterity saving throws against effects you can see, such as traps and spells (as long as you aren't blinded, deafened, or incapacitated).

Primal Path: Path of the Beast

Form of the Beast: When you enter your rage, you can manifest a natural weapon—a monstrous Bite.

Flavor: Your body has been "reformatted" for consumption. Your jaw unhinges and your teeth elongate into vicious fangs. This bite is a simple melee weapon that uses Strength. By consuming the flesh of your enemies in this way, you literally feast on their life force to mend your own wounds.

Background Feature (Criminal)

Criminal Contact: You have a reliable contact in the criminal underworld. On Cluster, this could be a corrupt guard or powerful inmate who runs the black market.

PERSONALITY & BACKGROUND

Backstory: Convicted of heresy, murder, and cannibalism—all in devotion to His Divine Shadow. Sentenced to organ harvesting on Cluster, she awaits her fate with barely contained hunger and rage.

Personality Note: Her rage is a "Feeding Frenzy"—a state of ecstatic, carnivorous violence where pain fades and only the tearing of flesh matters. At level 3, her body has been further "reformatted" by His Divine Shadow's dark blessing, allowing her to manifest horrific physical transformations when she enters her bloodlust.

"I hunger... and His Shadow hungers through me."
